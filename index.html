Welcome to Chokwenda Education Community- Empowering Self-Reliance

Purpose

The educational community serves to promote learning and exchange of information from around the world, to foster cross-cultural understanding, connections and learning techniques.

We support sustainable learning systems by advocating for sustainable practices, hence creating opportunities worldwide and becoming self reliant.

We want to encourage people to learn practical ways to live in different parts of the world.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: What features would be recommended so that people can succeed in learning to be self-reliant?
  • Scenario 2: How do people share their learning experiences so that they can motivate others?
  • Scenario 3: How can I manage my time effectively so that I can improve my living standards?
