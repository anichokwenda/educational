Welcome to Chokwenda Education Community

Purpose

The educational community serves to promote learning and exchange of information from around the world, to foster cross-cultural understanding, connections and learning techniques.

We support sustainable learning systems by advocating for sustainable practices, hence creating opportunities worldwide.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: What features would be recommended so that people can succeed in learning?
  • Scenario 2: How do people share their learning experiences so that they can motivate others?
  • Scenario 3: How can I manage my time effectively so that I can improve my learning?
